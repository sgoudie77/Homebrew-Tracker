JS Brewing History

JS Brew began in 1996 when two university students began brewing their own beer at home learning the basic steps and techniques from a local Brewery called Brewery Lane, as well as following advice from uncles and grandparents who had experience making homebrew beer.


The first few batches were simple lagers and went fairly well, so they decided to expand and try different types of beer kits and learn the craft in more detail, even going so far as to make their own recipes from scratch. With each new batch techniques and taste improved, and they discovered their signature brew was a Dry Cider. Popularity of their homebrew grew among their friends, and it became common at local gatherings, BBQs, and parties.


After refining their homebrew over 5 years and stockpiling a good supply of beer, the founders took a break for a number of years and resumed making homebrew in 2021 using their original method.
